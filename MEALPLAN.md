# Ranch House Meal Plan

## Trip Overview

- 10 people: 6 adults and 4 kids, ages 5-11
- Arrive Saturday evening
- Leave Monday mid-morning
- Priorities: simple meals, kid-friendly choices, flexible snacks, and minimal prep

## Saturday

### Dinner: Burgers and Hot Dogs

- Burgers and hot dogs
- Buns and basic toppings
- Chips
- Cut fruit or an easy fruit tray

### Evening Snacks

- Popcorn
- Chocolate chip cookies
- Individual vanilla/chocolate Blue Bell ice cream cups

## Sunday

### Breakfast: Make-Your-Own Breakfast Tacos

- Scrambled eggs
- Tortillas
- Breakfast sausage
- Shredded cheese
- Salsa and optional hot sauce
- Fruit

### Lunch: Sandwich Bar

- Deli meat and cheese sandwiches
- Peanut butter and jelly option for kids
- Chips
- Raw vegetables or fruit

### Afternoon Snacks

- Kid-friendly grab-and-go snacks
- Fruit
- Crackers or pretzels
- Optional cheese sticks

### Dinner: Taco Bar

- Seasoned ground beef
- Fajita chicken with onions and bell peppers
- Tortillas and/or tortilla chips
- Shredded cheese, lettuce, salsa, and sour cream
- Optional guacamole
- Black beans or refried beans
- Easy dessert

## Monday

### Breakfast: Easy Departure Breakfast

- Bagels or muffins
- Cream cheese and butter
- Yogurt
- Fruit
- Optional leftover breakfast taco ingredients

## Throughout the Trip

- Coffee and breakfast drinks
- Water
- Grab-and-go Apple & Eve apple juice boxes
- Lemon-lime (yellow) and red Gatorade
- Extra snacks for grazing between meals

## Decisions Before Building the Grocery List

- Choose the Saturday dinner side
- Confirm any dietary restrictions or strong preferences
- Confirm they have a grill
